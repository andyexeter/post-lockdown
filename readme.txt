Protects posts of any post type (except nav menu items) by disabling trashing/deletion and locks them by preventing edits by non-admins

== Description ==

Post Lockdown protects and optionally locks your site-critical posts and pages
by preventing non-admins from trashing or deleting them. They can also be locked completely which stops
any non-admins from being able to edit the post, regardless of their capability

== Installation ==

1. Upload `post-lockdown` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Choose your protected/locked pages under Settings > Post Lockdown
